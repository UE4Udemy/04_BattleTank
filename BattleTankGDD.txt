concept: 
Battle Tank is an open-world versus tank combat game
Terrain will be used for advantage ie high ground
Focus will be on feel ie only keep what's fun

rules:
Can move anywhere in terrain, surrounded by mountains
Both players start with pre determined health and ammo
Each direct hit takes health
Last player standing wins

requirements:
SFX: Gun firing, explosion, barrel moving, turret moving, engine sound
StaticMesh: Tank consisting of tracks, body, turret and barrel
Textures: Add later on for flare
Music: Background music to create tension
